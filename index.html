Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes.
  • Camels are large land mammals best known for their humps.
  • Camels ears are furry.

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons colors change according to their mood.
  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the Iguana Suborder.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons have great homing instincts.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals, and fly in big flocks.
  • Pigeons can fly at an average speed of 77.6 mph.

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day.
  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bears

  • Morris Michtom created the teddy bear.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The Teddy Bears’ Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Most cicadas have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
